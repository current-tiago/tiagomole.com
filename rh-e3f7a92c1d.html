Six Little Love Letters

tap a letter to break the seal and read it — fair warning, they're hopelessly soppy ♥

I
T♥I
Sample no.1 open me ♥

Light across the hallway floor

Emerging from an oft-closed door

Nervous now it is to shine

So brightly yet it awaits the time

A picture yet not fully known

Reflections of a thought ungrown

The time grows close at hand

Imagine the light bouncing off the sand

yours, before you even knew — T
II
T♥I
View of the Room open me ♥

Records dot your blanked out wall

You clear your breathe, stutter and stall

How clearly now I can see

The thoughts within that seek to flee

The vinyl turns, the words fall out

How red you turn a rose could sprout

And yet it renders me stuck and frozen

How glad I am of the path i've chosen

still frozen, still glad — T
III
T♥I
T-3 Weeks open me ♥

An orange hue colours your face

As red nails flow across the place

Pink lips flash as if to chase

The yellow Smints you lost to space

Blue eyes I profess I use to gaze

Upon the brown hair adorning your pretty face

Green with envy I admit to have been

At the golden bracelet adorning your skin

How gracefully smoothly your mouth contorts

Smiling already at the forthcoming retort

Almost proud enough to say

With your words you've kept me at bay

How happily now you sit and stare

When months before you would resort to glare

At the silence passing between your fingers

It feels as if the words now linger

every colour of you — T
IV
T♥I
Studying open me ♥

Not every smile, a thought

Yet not every glance is caught

Words unspoken but still persist

Dreams of mine I can't resist

Even as I sit and write

My mind is occupied by a sight

Hands in motion, looks exchanged

Clothes quickly become estranged

back to revising… eventually — T
V
T♥I
Orpheus open me ♥

I never understood Orpheus

And his inability to parry the gaze

Of the one he found himself attached to

In life and life again before the death

But now I recognise the thoughts he wondered

Imagining his woman torn asunder

He had to look one last time

At what I know to be mine

I'd turn around too — T
VI
T♥I
Summer Afternoon open me ♥

Oh how I wish to pass my days

Lingering long in your unbroken gaze

Sitting under the sun soaking

In all that you tell me that I was hoping

To hear from a girl as smart as you

And one so beautiful too

Truly I wished to have such luck

To have my heart oh so struck

your luckiest boy — T
