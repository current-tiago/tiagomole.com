The Summer Schedule

Four goals, protected in the slot that suits each best — Portuguese and writing while the mind is fresh, Juku at midday, slack in the afternoon, the gym to close the day.

Why It's Ordered This Way
Português first

Active study goes before Juku, not after — it's the thing most likely to be skipped when tired, so the morning protects it. Small daily touches beat one long session; a low-effort wind-down at night fills the cracks.

Writing while fresh

Real thinking needs an uninterrupted head. Mon/Wed research & outline · Tue/Thu draft · Fri edit & polish — so by Friday there's a finished piece to publish weekly, or hold one week for biweekly.

Juku, boxed

Even at a couple of hours, it gets a fixed midday block so it doesn't creep and swallow the day. The 14:30 slack is deliberate — fundraising calls, user chats and building rarely fit a neat box, so give the overflow room.

Gym anchors the evening

Placed at 19:00 on weekdays — late enough to suit you, early enough to avoid a midnight dinner. Weekends flip earlier around the shorter hours: Saturday closes 8pm, Sunday 3pm.
